Students are not required to take external entrance examinations such as GED, IELTS, TOEFL, IGCSE, Duolingo, or other admission tests. To pursue a 4-year Bachelor's Degree while working in Thailand, students must first complete a Foundation or Pathway Program.

The program offers an on-campus option in Yangon for students residing in Myanmar. For students currently living in Thailand, online classes are available. Since these are recorded video classes, you can conveniently study at your own pace and anytime at your convenience.

Thonburi University 🎓

To pursue a Bachelor's Degree while working in Bangkok:

📌 Admission Requirements

  • Old Curriculum Grade 9/10 completion, GED, or New Curriculum Grade 11 completion.
  • Completion of the 6-Month Foundation Program.

📚 Subjects Included

  • Business Management
  • Computer Studies
  • Thai Language
  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology

These core subjects will be delivered by experienced university professors.
*(On-campus learning is available at our Yangon center. For students based in Thailand, flexible online recorded video classes are provided, allowing you to study at your own convenience).*

💰 Program Fees

Total Tuition Fee: 39,500 THB

We provide a flexible installment plan as detailed below:

  • First Installment (Enrollment): 10,000 THB
  • Second Installment (Class Commencement): 10,000 THB
  • Remaining Balance: 19,500 THB (Payable over the following 2 months)

Upon successful completion of the Foundation Program, the university administration will provide comprehensive guidance and end-to-end support to assist students in securing employment while studying in Bangkok.

University Internship + Bachelor's Degree Program

Now you can pursue your University Degree while doing a Full-Time Internship in Bangkok…💼

Through our flexible program (attending classes one day per week alongside a full-time internship), you will enjoy the following benefits:

  • Financial Independence: Ability to fund your own tuition fees through your internship earnings.
  • Affordable Tuition: Highly competitive fees at only 34,000 THB per semester (with two semesters per academic year).
  • Flexible Installments: Option to pay tuition fees in installments throughout the duration of each semester.
  • Low-Cost Housing: Affordable university dormitory rooms starting around only 500 THB per month.
  • Official Internship Support: An official internship letter authorized by Royal Thai Immigration issued starting from the second semester of your First Year.
  • Legal Student Status: Full University Education Visa (ED Plus Visa + Re-entry Permit) coverage.
  • Post-Graduation Visa Extensions: A 1-year automatic visa extension upon completion of your 4-year Bachelor's degree to either pursue a Master's Degree or search for a corporate white-collar job.
  • Competitive Internship Stipend: Initial stipend starting at a minimum of 12,000 THB per month, often including accommodation and options for overtime (OT), bonuses, and tips, with salary increments evaluated every 3 months.
  • Bilingual Certification: A dual-language (Thai-English 50/50) curriculum, granting you professional proficiency in both languages upon graduation.
  • Global Qualification: A recognized degree that can be utilized to build a career in Thailand or to pursue further opportunities in third countries.
  • Intensive Language Training: Practical workplace-ready Thai language training fully delivered during your first semester.
  • Local Essential Setup: Seamless administrative assistance in opening local bank accounts, obtaining driving licenses, and securing health insurance.
  • Flexible Passport Types: Acceptance of multiple passport variants including PV and PE types.
  • Comprehensive Documentation: Full, end-to-end documentation and administrative support provided directly by the university for visas and related paperwork.

3 Pathways to Securing Your Internship

Upon reaching the second semester of your first academic year, every student will receive an official internship endorsement letter from Thonburi University to begin interviewing with prospective employers.

Pathway 1 (Direct Campus Interviews): HR managers and executives from companies partnering with Thonburi University under Memorandums of Understanding (MOUs) visit the campus directly to interview and recruit qualified students.

Pathway 2 (Senior Student Network): With a strong community of nearly 200 Myanmar students introduced through MRVT College, existing seniors frequently provide direct job referrals and give hiring priority to their incoming juniors.

Pathway 3 (Independent Walk-in Applications): Students can also independently approach corporations aligned with their career passions and apply directly utilizing their official university internship letters. This remains one of our most successful placement routes.

*To maximize these opportunities, students are highly encouraged to build proficiency in Thai and English languages, which are essential for professional communication in the local workforce.* 🤍
